Summary

Aadra is a versatile professional with 5 years of work experience across 3 continents working in the data-driven impact sector as a Product Manager and a Data. Aadra has worked with big data products involving computer vision, deep learning and machine learning in the food-energy-water nexus. Aadra is a cohesive team worker with strong problem solving and interpersonal skills in global markets. Currently, Aadra works as Product Manager in a satellite data analytics company developing end-to-end products for monitoring the credit performance of agricultural clients in the rural sector. Aadra focuses on platform development (mobile and web) aliasing frequently with clients to ensure the product alignment with user needs. Aadra has executed and managed high-value projects with large government vendors, donors and multi-lateral organizations. Aadra is a polyglot fluent in English, Hindi, Marathi, Tamil and Spanish.
