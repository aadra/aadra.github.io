Summary

Aadra is a product professional with 7 years of work experience across 3 continents working in the data-driven impact sector as a Product Manager and a Consultant. An Edwin C. Price Fellow at Columbia University Aadra focussed on evaluating the role of technology in defining and shaping the role of agricultural societies in emerging markets. Aadra is a cohesive team worker with strong problem solving and interpersonal skills in global markets.

Currently, Aadra is working as a Growth PM at Random Walk, an AI product and services company. Her journey spans Fortune 500 companies, high-growth startups, and SaaS platforms. With a strong foundation in analytics, machine learning, and user experience design, Aadra thrives at the intersection of technology, business strategy, and customer needs. She has shipped products used by thousands of users worldwide, from data visualization platforms to AI native micro-learning platforms that simplify employee engagement.

Aadra worked as Product Manager at an Identity Verification company managing client requirements for multiple identity verification products. In the past, Aadra conducted UI/UX research and helped build the content calendar for a B2C farmer advisory app, that now has 1 Million+ downloads from the Playstore. Aadra supported platform development mobile and web, aliasing frequently with clients to ensure product alignment with user needs for a satellite imagery analytics company. Aadra has executed and managed high-value projects with several organizations and businesses. Aadra is a polyglot able to communicate in English, Hindi, and Marathi.
