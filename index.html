Summary

Aadra is a product professional with 5 years of work experience across 3 continents working in the data-driven impact sector as a Product Manager and a Consultant. An Edwin C. Price Fellow at Columbia University Aadra focussed on evaluating the role of technology in defining and shaping the role of agricultural societies in emerging markets. Aadra has worked with big data products involving machine learning in the food-energy-water nexus. Aadra is a cohesive team worker with strong problem solving and interpersonal skills in global markets. Currently, Aadra works as Product Manager at a Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) company managing client requirements for multiple identity verification products. In the past, Aadra conducted UI/UX research and built the content calendar for a B2C farmer advisory app, that now has 1 Million+ downloads from the Playstore. At a satellite imagery analytics company, Aadra built end-to-end products for monitoring the credit risk of farmer profiles. Aadra supported platform development mobile and web, aliasing frequently with clients to ensure product alignment with user needs. Aadra has executed and managed high-value projects with several organizations and businesses. Aadra is a polyglot fluent in English, Hindi, Marathi, Tamil and Spanish.
